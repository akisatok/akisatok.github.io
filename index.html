Biography

Akisato Kimura is Senior Research Scientist & Supervisor at Media Information Laboratory, NTT Communication Science Laboratories. His research interests include media understanding, pattern recognition, machine learning, data mining and computer vision. He is provisionally surving as a human resource manager at Research Planning Section, NTT Communication Science Laboratories.
