Biography

Akisato Kimura is the group leader of Recognition Research Group at Media Information Laboratory, NTT Communication Science Laboratories. His research interests include media understanding, pattern recognition, machine learning, data mining and computer vision.

A spotlight paper to be presented in NeurIPS2020

We are excited to announce that our paper “Baxter permutation process” has been accepted to NeurIPS2020 as a spotlight presentation. In this paper, a Bayesian nonparametric model for Baxter permutations (BPs), termed BP process (BPP) is proposed and applied to relational data analysis.
